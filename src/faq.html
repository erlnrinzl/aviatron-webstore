Get answers to common questions and reach out to our support team for drone-related assistance.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I list my drone for sale on Aviatron?

To list your drone for sale, go to your Dashboard and click "Add Product". Fill in the necessary product details and submit.

What payment methods are accepted?

Aviatron supports major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for all transactions.

How does the rental process work?

Browse available drones, select rental duration, complete payment, and arrange delivery or pickup. Return in original condition.

What happens if a drone is damaged?

A security deposit covers potential damage. The owner assesses the issue and deducts repair costs as needed.

Do you offer shipping?

Sellers may offer shipping with clearly displayed options on each product listing. Local pickup may apply for larger drones.

What is your return policy?

Returns are available within 14 days if the product is defective or does not match the description. Contact the seller directly.
